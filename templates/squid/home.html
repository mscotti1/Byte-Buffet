Fried Calamari Recipe

Ingredients Instructions
- 1 lb cleaned squid 1. Whisk 1 cup of milk along with the garlic powder and cayenne.
- 1 cup condensed milk 2. cut into 1/2 inch rings, plus tentacles.
- 2 tsp cayenne pepper 3. Add the calamari rings and tentacles and mix well.
- 2 tsp garlic powder 4. Allow to marinate for 20 minutes.
- 2 tsp onion powder 5. Mix together the cornstarch and flour along with the onion powder and salt.
- kosher salt to taste 6. Dredge the calamari in the flour mixture.
- 3/4 cup flour 7. Fill a sauce pan 1/3 full with oil and heat to 350 degrees.
- 3/4 cup cornstarch 8. Fry the calamari in batches for 2-3 minutes or until golden.
9. Season with kosher salt and serve with a spicy marinara sauce for dipping.
