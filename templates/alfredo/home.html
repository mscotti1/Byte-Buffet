Chicken Alfredo Fettucini Recipe

Ingredients Instructions
- 6 Garlic cloves ; peeled and smashed with a knife 1. Rub garlic over chicken; reserve garlic. Season chicken with salt and pepper and rub in seasonings with your hands; set aside.
- 1 lb Boneless chicken breast ; washed, uncooked patted dry 2. Heat a large, heavy skillet over medium heat.
- 1 tspn Salt 3. Off heat, coat skillet with cooking spray and then set over medium-low heat. Sauté garlic until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add chicken and cook until golden, flipping once, about 5 minutes per side.
- 1 tspn Pepper 4. Heat a large skillet (nonstick or cast-iron) or griddle over medium. Add ½ tsp of butter.
- Spray Cooking Spray - Off heat, coat skillet with cooking spray and then set over medium-low heat. Sauté garlic until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add chicken and cook until golden, flipping once, about 5 minutes per side.
- 1/2 cup Canned Chicken Broth 6. Meanwhile, in a small bowl, combine broth, heavy cream and yogurt; pour over chicken and scrape bottom of skillet with a wooden spoon to incorporate seasonings. Simmer chicken gently, turning once, about 30 minutes.
- 1/4 cup Heavy whipping cream 7. While chicken is cooking, bring a medium pot of salted water to a boil. Cook pasta according to package directions; drain.
- 1/4 cup whole milk plain yogurt 8. Remove chicken from cream sauce and set aside. Add Parmesan cheese to cream sauce and mix until well-combined. Add cooked pasta and 3 tablespoons of parsley; toss well. Serve pasta topped which chicken and rest of parsley. Yields about 3 ounces of chicken and 2/3 cup of pasta/sauce per serving.
- 1/8 tspn table salt ; for cooking pasta 9. Notes: For a hint of spice, you can add 1/4 teaspoon of freshly grated nutmeg to the sauce. You can also use basil instead of parsley.
- 8 oz Whole wheat pasta fettuccini
- 1/2 cup Pramesan cheese ; Suggested Parmigiano-Reggiano
- 1/4 cup Fresh parsley chopped
